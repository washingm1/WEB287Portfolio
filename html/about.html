Where I'm From

My family moved from New Jersey to North Carolina in 2007 when I was 4 years old. Since I had not been in New Jersey for a long time, the move here was not a big shock. As I grow older, and my town in North Carolina becomes more populated, I begin to appreciate the quietness and slower pace that we had when first moving here. This more relaxed lifestyle has become less prevalent here, but I find that keeping that same attitude regardless of your surroudings helps keep you less stressed in daily life.
